[Polarius](https://brotherhood42.github.io) is a Matrix communication client that combines a proof-of-contribution token system for shared goals with contribution-weighted governance. Through this, we can regenerate the driving force of communities to promote a borderless mutual aid society while overcoming the limitations of existing national systems.

## Existing National Systems

The equivalent exchange of 'contribution-guarantee' between members and communities is a core principle of community maintenance and operation. However, due to structural flaws in modern society, the guarantee of members' contributions is not practically realized.

### Credit-based Issuance Structure: Loss of Distribution Rights for Contributions

In today's financial system, currency is issued through a credit creation process based on central banks' reserve ratios, mostly through real estate loans. The main path for new currency circulation is not 'substantial contribution' to the community like production activities, but a 'credit loan structure' secured by real estate, which is a necessity for life. The increase in currency volume in this issuance structure leads to price increases accompanied by necessities, ultimately burdening citizens with 'rising survival costs'. This creates a gap between gradual wage increases and prices, and members gradually lose their distribution rights for contributions, eventually facing economic recession and asset market collapse. In this economic structure, the driving force base of actual contributors cannot be maintained, so the community's positive feedback structure cannot be secured. This applies not only to the existing central bank's credit-based currency issuance structure but also to most cryptocurrencies that are issued without being based on actual contributions.

### Authority-centered Governance Structure: Absence of Governance Rights for Contributions

For the efficiency of decision-making, members elect representatives for governance and delegate governance rights to representatives. However, political representatives have inefficiencies in that they have slow and insufficient information about all members, making it impossible to provide accurate and timely feedback on problems. The delegated feedback is distorted by party politics, causing greater market problems. The essence of system paralysis is not the majority of citizens who form the system and need it, but a minority governance structure separated from them. Human communities with paralyzed political negative feedback structures face repeated social collapse.

## Polarius, New System

Polarius issues currency based on proof of contribution to the community, ensuring members' contribution distribution rights. In this issuance structure, currency holdings are quantified contribution amounts, and point-fixed governance is implemented for holdings to ensure contribution governance rights. All of this operates on a decentralized server structure on the Matrix communication protocol.

### User Guide: Proof-of-Contribution Issuance Structure

Anyone can form autonomous organizations with common goals on the Polarius client, and anyone can receive currency issuance through the proof-of-contribution function for common goals in communication rooms within the formed organization. This way, currency is issued directly to contribution subjects, preserving members' contribution driving force, and communities secure sustainability along with positive feedback structures.

### User Guide: Contribution-based Governance Structure

Currency holdings in the proof-of-contribution issuance structure are contribution amounts. The Polarius client supports Snapshot governance, a point-fixed governance function for currency holdings, ensuring members' contribution governance rights. This way, all participants are guaranteed equality of rights, implementing a reasonable social contract where "community contributors are community decision-making participants."

## To the Brothers of Anarchy

What is the state?

Humanity has turned its back on the mutual aid of all things' cooperation, and through the false principle of development called infinite competition, it has fallen into becoming components for the state.

Social evolutionary competition refers to competition between life 'systems' that maintain connection with nature, not competition between individuals themselves.

But look at our empire. Look at the 'empty' empire of kings who do not know humans, do not know love, and are deficient in it.

While humans create civilization, under the pretext of civilization's development, human exploitation is taken for granted, and while buildings rise higher and higher, the flames in our hearts grow infinitely smaller.

Science and technology speak of the 'future,' but we are increasingly trying to find answers in things of the past.

We speak of 'freedom,' but we are increasingly trying to find meaning of existence within the system.

What is civilization for, and what is the system for? Have we forgotten that the root of Homo sapiens surviving through long history and creating civilization was 'cooperation'? The heart of one for all, all for one is precisely that, and we must not forget that this is the 'system.'

In the past, we had no alternative, but now it is different.

The history of decentralization that began with the internet and was imprinted by Bitcoin is only about a decade long, but in that short time, essential concepts of community such as 'DAO' have emerged.

This concept does not simply mean technological advancement but symbolizes a fundamental shift in thinking about the relationship between systems and humans.

What we must do is clear. We must not stop at viewing this flow of change as mere technological innovation, but we must correctly apply it and put a period on the transition to a 'new social system.'

If we use this as an axis and manifest community bonds so that everyone comes together with one will, we can achieve a 'revolution in the true sense' that is completed through voluntary participation, trust, and shared vision, not through violence or coercion.

All for one, One for all.  
That is the truth,  
and 'love' is precisely that.
